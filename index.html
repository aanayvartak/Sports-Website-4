What is corona virus

A corona virus is a kind of a commom virus that causes an infection in your nose, sinuses, or upper hroat. Most corona virus aren't dangerous.

In early 2020, after december 2019 outbreak in China, the World Health Organization identified SARS-CoV-2 as a new type of corona virus. The outbreak Quickly spread around the world.

COVID-19 is a disese cuased by SARS-Cov-2 that can trigger what doctors call a respiratory tract infection. It affect your upper respiratory tract(sinuses, nose, and throat) or lower respiratory tract(windpipe)

It spreads the same way other coronaviruses do, mainly through person-to-person contact. Infections range from mild to deadly.

SARS-CoV-2 is one of the seven types ofcorona virus, including the ones that cause severe diseases like Middle East respiratory syndrone (MERS) and sudden acute respiatory syndrone (SARS).
